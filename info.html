About TinyQuesadilla

Hi! We're TinyQuesadilla, a tiny remote game development studio based out of Columbus and Indianapolis, making games both physical and digital.

Imagined in 2018 and officially founded in the metaphorical fires of 2020, we are a small team of artists, graphic designers, illustrators, programmers, and gamers.

We're just here to have a good time and make stuff that we would play, because it's fun.

Wren Eli Grey-Hayes

Programming, Copywriting, Narrative and Game Design

A Neopets veteran, Tamagotchi parent, and frequent nostalgic for the halcyon days of EverQuest2, Wren drinks cold brew coffee like their life depends on it. By day, they are a database administrator, and at night, they cook and then they mess around with code until it works. They like to see how long they can go without using a mouse, how much of a job can be automated, and how often they can get away with using shatterdive. Their favorite programming language is Python, their favorite IDE is VSCode, and their favorite place to write stories is at a table with friends and half-off appetizers. Find Wren on LinkedIn here.

Robyn K. Hayes

Art, Illustration, Assets and Narrative Consultation

Robyn has always loved drawing, and eventually got really good at it, and now everyone asks her to draw stuff for them. Robyn loves milk tea, plants, and all things miniature, and she keeps a great many Twitter patrons happy with thoughtful, heartfelt art of original and established characters. She got into Graphic Design professionally a few years ago after getting her degree in it, but is working towards a full-time career in the games industry. Her current setup includes a Samsung tablet, a spot on the floor in front of a heater, and a delightful array of fan merchandise for games like Destiny 2, Portal, and Death Stranding.
