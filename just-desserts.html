Just Desserts

A delightful pastel gingham trip around the world awaits you in this picnic basket box containing a family-friendly card game, inspired by our favorite baking competition shows. Place dice to assign time and ingredients to recipes, and present your finished products to an array of judges, each with their own tastes and style. Learn about a global variety of baked goods, sabotage your fellow bakers, and create a piece du resistance that will blow everyone away to win in this game of cards and dice.

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Age: 7+
  • Contains 50 cards with unique illustrations by Robyn Hayes
