TinyWorld

TinyWorld is a browser-based love letter to the virtual pet websites of the early 2000s, built in Django. The beta is planned to launch in December of 2022. Sign up for the beta here!

The site includes features such as:
  • creating, playing with, and feeding up to 4 pets
  • exploring 4 fun themed worlds
  • playing through 4 different click-through "choose your own adventure" games to unlock parts of the site
  • earning site currency through daily clickable activities
  • spending site currency to buy toys and food for your pet
  • ... and more to come!
